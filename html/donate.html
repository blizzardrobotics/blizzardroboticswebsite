Support the next generation of STEM engineers.

Blizzard runs entirely on crowdfunding and sponsors, donations of any amount go a long way.

Blizzard would not be possible without support from kind individuals who are willing to give back to education in their community. We greatly appreciate donations of any amount! All donations will be used to purchase hardware for our robot, register the team for competitions, and fund our COVID-19 initiatives.

If you would like to make a donation in some other, non-monetary form, don't hesitate to email us or contact us via chat! If you are from an organization or are interested in sponsoring Blizzard Robotics, please reach out to us via chat or email blizzard.robotics@gmail.com.
